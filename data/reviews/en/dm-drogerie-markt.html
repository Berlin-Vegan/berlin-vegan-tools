“dm Drogeriemarkt”, a drugstore chain, offers quite a big range of vegan products. Especially their own brand “Alverde” includes body lotions, shampoos, and any body care products, most of them vegan and even with the Vegan Society’s logo on the packaging.

Also a few of the DM products by “Balea” are 100% vegan, and all of them were not tested on animals.

Regarding food, DM also sells organic and vegan products of the brand “Alnatura”, a big offer at a convenient price including soy milk, tofu, spreads and patés, sweets, flours, sugar, spices and sauces.
