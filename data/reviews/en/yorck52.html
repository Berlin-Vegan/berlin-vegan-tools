A nice little vegan organic café was opened in June 2010: York52. The contains fair trade coffee and tea along with tasty cakes, wafers and salty snacks like bagels, rolls, antipasti and salads. Additionally, every day a different cooked dish is served at lunch time.

For the very young vegans there is a childrens play area.

Every Monday a co-working brunch takes place to get to know people, to network and to plan projects.

You will find further information on the café and the events as well as the answer to the question what is a co-working space? at Yorck52.de.
